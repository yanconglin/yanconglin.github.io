Research

I am enthusiastic in data efficient learning by pre-wiring deep networks with generic geometric priors. My research is about re-parameterizing old-school vision techniques into end2end modules, where the geometric knowledge does not need to be learned from big data. I have been working on 2D/3D scene parsing from low-level geometric structures, such as line segments, vanishing points and symmetry planes. The ultimate goal is to better understand visual scenes without relying on large manually labeled data. I also have experience in implementing real-time 3D video capturing system.
